# Northstar Analytics Remote Work Policy

**Document ID:** HR-014  
**Owner:** People Operations  
**Version:** 2.1  
**Effective date:** March 1, 2026  
**Applies to:** United States employees approved for hybrid or remote work

## 1. Eligibility

Employees become eligible to request a remote or hybrid arrangement after
completing 90 calendar days of employment. A manager and People Operations must
approve the arrangement in writing. Approval depends on role responsibilities,
performance, team coverage, and information-security requirements.

Interns and employees on an active performance improvement plan are not eligible
for fully remote arrangements. A manager may approve occasional work from home
for these employees.

## 2. Hybrid Office Attendance

Hybrid employees must work from their assigned Northstar office at least two days
per week. Each department selects one required team day. The second office day
may be selected by the employee with manager approval.

Employees may temporarily miss an office day because of illness, approved PTO,
business travel, severe weather, or an office closure. Missing an office day for
another reason requires manager approval.

## 3. Working Location

An approved remote arrangement applies only to the employee's documented primary
working location. Working from another U.S. state for more than 10 consecutive
business days requires advance approval from People Operations and Tax.

Employees may not work outside the United States without written approval from
People Operations, Legal, Tax, and Information Security. Approval should be
requested at least 30 calendar days before the proposed travel.

## 4. Equipment and Internet

Northstar provides one laptop, one monitor, a keyboard, a mouse, and a headset
for approved remote employees. Equipment remains company property. Employees
must return equipment within five business days after employment ends.

Northstar reimburses up to $60 per month for home internet used for work. A
receipt or billing statement is required. Internet reimbursements are submitted
under the "Remote Work - Internet" expense category and are not counted toward
the home-office setup allowance.

Approved remote employees may claim a one-time home-office setup allowance of up
to $500 during their first year in the arrangement. Furniture, an additional
monitor, and ergonomic accessories are eligible. Decorations, rent, utilities,
and recurring coworking memberships are not eligible.

## 5. Security Requirements

Employees must use the company VPN when accessing internal systems from a public
or shared network. Confidential conversations must not be conducted where they
can be overheard. Lost or stolen equipment must be reported according to SEC-003.

## 6. Review and Revocation

Managers review remote arrangements at least annually. Northstar may change or
revoke an arrangement based on business needs, performance, security concerns,
or failure to follow this policy.
