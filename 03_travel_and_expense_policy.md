# Northstar Analytics Travel and Expense Policy

**Document ID:** FIN-008  
**Owner:** Finance  
**Version:** 4.0  
**Effective date:** February 1, 2026  
**Applies to:** Employees and approved contractors incurring business expenses

## 1. General Rules

Expenses must have a clear business purpose, be reasonable, and include required
documentation. Employees must submit expense reports within 30 calendar days of
the transaction or trip end date, whichever is later.

An itemized receipt is required for any expense of $25 or more. Finance may
request documentation for expenses below $25. Splitting a purchase to avoid the
receipt requirement is prohibited.

## 2. Travel Approval and Booking

Domestic overnight travel requires manager approval before booking. International
travel also requires approval from the relevant vice president and the Legal
team. Flights and hotels must be booked through Northstar's approved travel
portal unless the portal is unavailable or Finance grants an exception.

Employees should book economy airfare. Premium economy is allowed for a flight
segment longer than six hours. Business-class airfare requires written approval
from the Chief Financial Officer before booking.

## 3. Lodging and Ground Transportation

Hotel costs should not exceed $275 per night before taxes in standard-cost
cities or $375 per night before taxes in designated high-cost cities. The travel
portal identifies high-cost cities. A manager may approve a higher rate when a
conference hotel or safety requirement makes it necessary.

Employees should select the lowest reasonable ground-transportation option.
Rental cars require manager approval. Traffic fines, parking fines, and vehicle
upgrades are not reimbursable.

## 4. Meals

Employees traveling overnight may claim actual meal costs up to $75 per day,
including tips. Itemized receipts are required when an individual meal costs $25
or more. Alcohol is reimbursable only when purchased with a customer meal and
approved by the most senior Northstar employee attending.

## 5. Remote-Work Expenses

Monthly internet reimbursement and the one-time home-office setup allowance are
governed by HR-014. Employees submit those expenses through the expense system,
but the eligibility rules and limits in HR-014 control.

## 6. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

Northstar does not reimburse personal entertainment, minibar purchases, commuting
between home and an assigned office, childcare, pet care, airline lounge
memberships, or expenses incurred by a companion.

## 7. Exceptions

Policy exceptions require written Finance approval before the expense is
incurred. Manager approval alone does not authorize reimbursement of a prohibited
expense.
